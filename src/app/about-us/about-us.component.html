About Cuttle Card

The Cuttle credit card is an asset-based credit card. Customers can use their pledged assets to make purchases, go shopping, and use ATM advances to withdraw cash. The Master card global network makes it simple and convenient to make payments, whether it's for online shopping or signing bills abroad, once the cardholder has injected the secured assets.
